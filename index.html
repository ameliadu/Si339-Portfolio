Sheer Tops

Sheer Top

Brands like For Love And Lemons have made sheer tops trendy casual clothing, rather than the traditional undergarment.

Embroidery

Embroidery

This ancient practice has come back into style time and time again. This intricate detail that is required with this practice brings back constant appreciation for clothing crafts men and women

Pastels

Pastels

Every spring pastels reappear in our stores. These reminders of the sunshine and flowers that boost happiness in spring and summer are the very definition of how clothing can influence moods.

Leather Jackets

Leather Jacket

Leather, and leather jackets, come in and out of Fashion every few years. This season statement leather, like this one, are definers among fashion and instagram influencers.

Statement

Statement Piece

Recently the LA brands EB Denim and Revice has redifined the way we see blue jeans. Their use of vintage denim and embroidery has made blue jeans a statement piece, rather than a mundane classic.

Monogram

Monogram

Alessandro Michele was the first to turn monograms from tacky to classy. His use of the Gucci logo has redifined luxury fashion and has become a statement of luxury in today's millenials.

Cut Out

Cutout Dress

Subtle and strategically placed cutouts have replaced the classic mini dress. Now the new sultry is in small fragments of skin and/or sheer linings.

Colored Prints

Colored prints

This season is all about new and bold. Refurbished appreciations for trained artists have resurfaced in the use of their bold prints and artwork within clothing. The trend is known as wearable art.

Head Scarves

Head Scarves

These accessories have entered the main stream fashion scene with a variety of prints to pick from. However, they have spewed controversy because of cultural approptiation accusations as well as double standards for white women that are not granted to those of color when wearing these scarves.

Fashion is one of the most popular modes of expression in today's society It expresses different cultures, ages, occupations, and trends.
Here I wanted to describe the way I choose to express myself in clothing and pieces I'd like to wear.
